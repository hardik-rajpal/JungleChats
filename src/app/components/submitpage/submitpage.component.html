Welcome to KnowYourTube!

This website provides tools to analyze ('analyse' if you've been conditioned to hold out your pinky while sipping hot water) your consumption of the well known video hosting platform.

If you are familiar with Google Takeout, proceed to open the page on the right to download your latest history from YouTube.

Open Google Takeout Page

If not, follow the simple 2-minute tutorial below.
Note: The program analyzes only your Watch History from YouTube. Therefore, it does not require any data from the other apps, or even from YouTube.

On requesting the takeout, you will receive two emails from Google almost immediately (unless you've been watching a whole lot of YouTube ). The second one contains the link to download a zip file containing your data. Locate the watch-history.html file and add it to the form below. Press Submit and you're good to go!
